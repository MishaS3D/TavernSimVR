===INTRO TITLE SCREEN===

For the first time in your life, you slowly descend the stairs into the building. You move hesitantly and with trepidation, as if you don’t belong here—even though you own the place.

You step off the last stair, pass the threshold, and behold the tavern your father bequeathed you: The Woolyford Arms.

You didn’t even know he was alive. Why would he leave you this tavern? How did he end up owning a tavern to begin with?

You reach into your satchel and pull out the letter that upended your life. You read it, one more time:

“Dear Naomi, you may not remember me, but I haven't forgotten about you for even a second. I am Alonso Steele, your father. And if you are reading this, it means I am dead. I am sorry I never wrote to you or came to visit. I had my reasons, but I can assure you none of them were due to me not loving or being interested in you. Not a day passed by without me thinking of you. And now, I bequeath to you my tavern in the city: The Woolyford Arms. 

I don't know why, but I feel you'll do better with this place than I ever did. Build it up, tear it down, sell it, rip up the deed--it makes no difference to me. But you've always been a headstrong, persistent, and clever girl. If anyone can run this place... I know it's you. I'm sorry you had to find all of this out like this. And I'm sorry for putting everything on you like this. Most of all, I'm sorry I couldn't be there for you. Both today, and every other day.

All my love, Dad.”

You sign, put the letter back in your satchel, and survey the empty space, trying to get a sense of the layout before the morning’s first customers come in.
